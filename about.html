About Us

BBN CORPORATION.

Established in year of 2023, BBN Corporation is one of the leading distribution Organization of PCB Assembly consumables and we support equipment for Auto Insertion Machine & Machine Parts (A I Machine Parts), SMT (Pick and Place Machine) Machine Parts, Wire Harness Machine (Wire Cutting / Stripping / Twisting / Crimping Machine) , Component Forming Machines, ESD Items, Lubricant (Lithium-Based Grease, Pneumatic Oil, High Temp Oil.) and Other consumable Items … The entire range of products offered by us is very popular in the markets for their afford ability and high durability nature. We focused vision to bring the top-quality products and brand from all over the world for the India Electronics Manufacturing Industry. Our ability to change and improve faster to support changing customer requirements. We thank you to our customers as well as our suppliers for helping us to become a well trusted and respected organization, providing world class products to Indian Electronics Manufacturing Industry.
